Itinerary

What to Expect

Stop 1 · Batuan Temple

A village temple decorated beautifully with sculptures and other types of ancient stone — a quiet first look at Balinese architecture before the day's adventures begin.

Stop 2 · Tegenungan Waterfall

Enjoy the impressively green valley panorama with cascading water as a centerpiece — one of Bali's most photogenic waterfalls.

Stop 3 · Coffee Plantation

Experience a coffee tour and try local coffee and tea, including Bali's famous Kopi Luwak, amid a lush garden setting.

Stop 4 · Tegalalang Rice Terrace

Exotic rice fields located in a beautiful valley — the iconic emerald terraces of Ubud, best seen in the soft morning light.

Stop 5 · Sacred Ubud Monkey Forest

The natural habitat of the Balinese long-tailed monkey, followed by an hour of free time to explore central Ubud, the Palace and the market.
